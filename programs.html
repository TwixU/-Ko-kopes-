How We Help

Our multifaceted approach to saving lives and supporting shelters.

Financial Support for Shelters

Many animal shelters operate on shoestring budgets. We provide financial assistance for utilities, facility repairs, or expansion. We mobilize funds to address urgent needs, like new kennels for a local dog shelter.

Charity Fundraising Auctions

We organize online auctions where supporters donate items to bid on. Proceeds go directly to animal welfare programs or specific shelters. It's a fun way to bring the community together for a good cause.

Providing Food and Supplies

We ensure animals never go hungry. We run drives to gather pet food, blankets, and cleaning materials, distributing them to overcrowded shelters and foster carers. A bag of food can be a lifesaver.

Veterinary Care Program

We cover or subsidize medical expenses, from emergency surgeries to routine vaccinations. Working with compassionate vets, we stretch every dollar to treat broken legs, infections, and more.

Adoption & Education

We promote "Don't Shop, Adopt!" and the importance of spaying/neutering. Through campaigns and workshops, we aim to create a more compassionate community informed about responsible pet ownership.

Emergency Rescue & Foster

In urgent cases, we step in to rescue and foster animals temporarily when shelters are full. We maintain a small network of foster homes to ensure no animal is left stranded.

Measuring Our Impact

Every program is driven by outcomes. Over the years, Kočkopes has:

  • Raised crucial funds preventing shelter closures.
  • Delivered tons of pet food and supplies.
  • Paid for life-saving surgeries for countless pets.
  • Increased adoption rates through our campaigns.
